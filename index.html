How to play

Goal Fly in from deep space, hook the highlighted cargo, and deliver it to the matching load zone.
Controls Use WASD or arrow keys on desktop. On mobile, steer with the left pad and use the right-side buttons.
Watch out Shooting clears hazards, but each destroyed object splits into two smaller problems.
Upgrade After each delivery, spend credits on hook range, line strength, handling, or ship paint.
